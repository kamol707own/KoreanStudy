## Key Grammar Points

### The Imperative Mood

There are multiple ways to give commands in Korean, from least formal to most formal:

### Verb stem + ~아/어(요)

The easiest and least formal way to give a command:

- 밥을 천천히 먹어 = Eat your food slowly
- 열심히 공부해! = Study hard!
- Add **~요** for politeness: 밥을 천천히 먹어요 = Eat your food slowly

### Verb stem + ~(으)세요

The most common formal imperative form (equivalent to ~(으)셔요):

- 이것을 보세요 = Look at this
- 빨리 올라오세요 = Come up quick
- 수고하세요! = Work hard! (common greeting when leaving a business)
- **안녕히 계세요** = Goodbye (stay in peace)

### Verb stem + ~아/어라

Used by older people (parents/grandparents) when giving orders to younger people:

- 빨리 가라! = Go fast!
- 내 말을 들어라 = Listen to what I say

> **Note:** This form is similar in formality to ~아/어 but sounds more authoritative.

### Verb stem + ~(으)십시오

Very formal imperative, used when speaking to people who deserve extreme respect:

- 여기서 내리십시오 = Get off here (heard in Seoul subway)

### Negative Imperative Mood: ~지 말다

Tell someone not to do something:

- 가지 마! = Don't go!
- 가지 마세요! = Don't go! (polite)
- 집에 가지 마! = Don't go home!
- 권력을 남용하지 마세요 = Don't abuse your power/authority

> **Note:** 말다 is irregular in imperative conjugations: 가지 말아, 가지 마라, 가지 마요, 가지 마세요, 가지 마십시오

### ~지 말고

Connect a negative command with a positive command (don't do X, but do Y):

- 매일 같은 운동을 하지 말고 많이 쉬세요 = Don't do the same exercise every day, and get lots of rest

### Giving Directions

- 오른 쪽으로 가세요 = Go right
- 왼 쪽으로 가세요 = Go left
- 직진하세요 = Go straight
- 건물을 지나가서 오른 쪽으로 가세요 = Go past the building, then go right

### The Imperative Mood with ~아/어 보다

Add ~아/어 보다 to imply "try doing something":

- 이것을 먹어 봐! = Try eating this! (or simply "Eat this!")
- 여기 와 보세요 = Come here (Try coming here)
- 문을 열어 봐 = Open the door (Try opening the door)

## Example Sentences

- 부끄러워하지 마세요 = Don't be shy
- 오늘도 수고한 자신에게 "잘했다!"라고 칭찬해주세요 = Today as well, praise yourself by saying "you did good!"
- 건물을 지나가서 오른 쪽으로 가지 말고 계속 직진하세요 = Go past the building, then don't turn right, but keep going straight
- 이 제품 수준을 높여 보세요 = Try to raise the level/standard of this product
- 여기서 내리십시오 = Get off here
