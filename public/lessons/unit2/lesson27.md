### Introduction

In the previous lesson, you learned how **~는 것** can:
- Turn clauses/sentences into nouns to make sentences like: **저는 친구가 사과를 가져오는 것을 원해요** = I want my friend to bring apples
- Use verbs to describe nouns to make sentences like: **저는 과학을 좋아하는 여자들을 좋아해요** = I like girls who like Science

Now, let's build on what you learned and apply the **~는 것** principle in other ways.

### Describe Past Tense with ~던 and ~았/었던

You can describe upcoming nouns in the past tense using **~던** and **~았/었던**.

**~던** indicates that the speaker is recalling/remembering some fact from the past that was experienced. It also indicates that an action occurred repeatedly in the past and may still be occurring.

For example:
- **내가 입은 옷** = The clothes I wore (Simple fact)
- **내가 입던 옷** = The clothes I recall wearing (Repeated action, still worn)
- **내가 입었던 옷** = The clothes I had worn (Action finished, no longer worn)

**Key differences:**
- **~ㄴ/은**: Simple past-tense fact. No additional meaning.
- **~던**: Recalled memory, action occurred repeatedly, may still be occurring.
- **~았/ờ었던**: Recalled memory, action finished and no longer occurs.

**Examples:**
- **내가 읽던 책은 재미없었어** = The book I was reading wasn't funny
- **이 빵은 내가 자주 먹던 빵이야** = This bread is bread that I ate often
- **제가 매일 마시던 주스를 샀어요** = I bought the juice that I drink everyday
- **2015년에 쓰던 달력을 내일 버릴 거예요** = Tomorrow, I am going to throw out the calendar that I used to use in 2015

**With ~았/었던:**
- **그 컴퓨터는 작년에 썼던 거예요** = That is the computer I had used last year
- **저는 애기가 썼던 휴지를 다 버렸어요** = I threw out all the tissue paper that the baby had used
- **한때 포기했던 시험을 다시 공부했어요** = I again studied for the exam that I once gave up on

### Describing Past Tense with ~던 and ~았/었던 (Adjectives)

**~았/었** can also be added to adjectives to indicate that the noun was that way in the past, but is no longer that way anymore.

**Example:**
- **예뻤던 여자** = The girl who was pretty (but is not pretty anymore)

**~던** can be added to adjectives but is less common. It indicates the adjective was true until recently.

**Examples:**
- **시끄럽던 교실** = The classroom that was noisy until now
- **따뜻하던 날씨** = The weather that was warm until now
- **맑던 하늘** = The sky that was clear until now

**Example sentences:**
- **맑던 하늘이 갑자기 어두워졌어요** = The sky that was clear until now suddenly became dark
- **따뜻하던 날씨가 비가 오고 나서 추워졌어요** = The weather that was warm until now got cold after it rained
- **조용하던 길이 사람들이 집에서 나오기 시작하고 나서 시끄러워졌어요** = The street that was quiet until now got loud after people started coming out of their houses
