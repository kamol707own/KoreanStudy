## Almost:幾乎

거의 is a common adverb meaning "almost":

**1) Showing an action has almost reached its limit:**
- 저는 밥을 거의 다 먹었어요 = I almost ate all the rice / I have almost eaten all the rice
- 저는 숙제를 거의 다 했어요 = I almost finished all of my homework
- 우리는 거의 다 왔어요 = We have almost arrived
- 박사 학위 공부를 거의 다 했어요 = I have almost finished my Ph.D.

**2) Showing something is just a tad off:**
- 저는 화장품이 거의 없어요 = I almost have no cosmetics
- 컴퓨터 때문에 많은 지식이 일상에서 거의 중요하지 않아요 = Because of computers, a lot of intelligence is almost not important in everyday life

## Almost: 거의… ~ㄹ/을 뻔 했다

~ㄹ/을 뻔 했다 describes an action that "almost" happened but never started and will not happen:

- 저는 거의 넘어질 뻔 했어요 = I almost fell
- 저는 차를 거의 칠 뻔 했어요 = I almost hit that car
- 화가 너무 나서 친구를 거의 때릴 뻔 했어요 = Because I was so mad, I almost punched my friend

> **Note:** The word "거의" doesn't need to be included since the meaning of "almost" is embedded in ~ㄹ/을 뻔 하다.

More examples:
- 거미를 보고 죽을 뻔 했어요 = I saw a spider and then almost died
- 우리는 1등급을 받을 뻔 했어요 = We almost received the first ranking
- 반항아가 선생님에게 침을 뱉을 뻔 했어요 = The rebel almost spat at the teacher
- 우리 사이가 어긋나서 싸울 뻔 했어요 = We don't get along well, so we almost fought
- 살인자가 사람을 죽인 동기를 말할 뻔 했어요 = The murderer almost said his motive for killing the person
