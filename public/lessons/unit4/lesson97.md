## An abbreviation of 가지다: 갖다

 갖다 is an abbreviated form of 가지다 (to have). It can replace 가지다 in all cases where it's used with 있다 or other verbs.

### Rules for 갖다

It is **incorrect** to add any grammatical principle starting with a vowel (or with the option of adding a vowel) to 갖다.

**Acceptable:**
- **갖고** (가지고)
- **갖는** (가지는)
- **갖지** (가지지)

**Incorrect:**
- ~~갖아/갖어~~ (가져)
- ~~갖았다/갖었다~~ (가졌다)

### Examples

- **용기를 갖고 힘내세요!** = Get some courage, and good luck!
- **저는 지난 주에 읽던 책을 마침내 갖고 왔어요** = I finally brought the book I was reading last week

---

## Because: ~아/어 가지고

~아/어 가지고 means "because" — very colloquial and common in speech.

- **어제 늦게 끝나 가지고 집에 못 갔어요** = Because I finished late yesterday, I couldn't go home
- **오늘 날씨가 너무 더워 가지고 약속을 취소했어요** = Because the weather is so hot today, I cancelled my plans

---

## 머물다, 서둘다, 서툴다

These are abbreviated forms of 머무르다, 서두르다, and 서투르다.

**Rule:** Grammatical principles starting with a vowel (and no other option) **cannot** be added to these shortened forms.

**Incorrect:**
- ~~머물어(요)~~ (머물렀어요)
- ~~머물었어(요)~~ (머물렀어요)
