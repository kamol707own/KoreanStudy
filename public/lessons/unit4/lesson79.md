## As Experienced: 대로

대로 is described by a clause to indicate that some action is done in the same way or form as one "experiences" an event.

### Common Constructions

- **제가 봤던 대로 / 제가 본 대로** = As I saw
- **제가 들었던 대로 / 제가 들은 대로** = As I heard
- **제가 느꼈던 대로 / 제가 느낀 대로** = As I felt
- **제가 예상했던 대로 / 제가 예상한 대로** = As I expected

### Examples

- **그 영화는 제가 예상한 대로 재미있었어요** = As I expected, that movie was funny
- **제가 들은 대로 이 지역 호박은 진짜 맛있어요** = As I heard, the pumpkins in this area are very delicious
- **내가 시키는 대로 해 줘** = Please do it as I ask

### Common Adverbs with 대로

- **그대로** = for something to be done as it once was
- **마음대로** = to do something as one's heart desires
- **순서대로** = in order
- **예상대로** = as expected

## Popular: 인기

- **인기가 많다** = to be popular
- **인기를 끌다** = to get popular
- **인기를 얻다** = to gain in popularity
- **인기가 떨어지다** = for popularity to fall

---

## To be Obvious: 당연하다

- **당연해요!** = Of course!
- **당근!** = Of course! (modern slang, literally "carrot")

### Examples

- **그가 그 여자를 사랑하는 것은 당연해요** = It is obvious that he loves that girl
- **그 사람이 지난 1년 동안 아주 열심히 일해서 그가 승진되는 게 당연한 거예요** = That person worked very hard, so it is obvious that he is getting promoted

---

## Appropriate: 알맞다

Used when two things go well with each other. Similar to 어울리다.

- **저 책상은 이 방에 알맞아요** = That desk looks good in this room

---

## The Whole: 전체

전체 means "the whole" of something. Place after a noun to refer to the entire noun.

- **학교 전체** = the whole school
- **도시 전체** = the whole city
- **회사 전체** = the whole company

---

## That is why: 바로 그 때문이다

- **제가 가고 싶은 것은 바로 그 때문이에요** = That is why I want to go
- **그래서 가고 싶어요** = That is why I want to go (more natural)
