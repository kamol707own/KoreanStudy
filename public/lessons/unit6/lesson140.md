# Lesson 140: ~(으)로서 – With a Specific Status

## Vocabulary

- **주장** = leader
- **장군** = a military general
- **검사** = prosecutor
- **효도** = kind acts towards one's parents
- **효자** = a devoted son
- **효녀** = a devoted daughter
- **임무** = duty
- **새언니** = a female's brother's wife (sister in law)
- **명절** = one of the two major holidays in Korea (Chuseok and/or Seolnal)
- **대리** = a substitute, a proxy
- **진품** = a real, genuine piece of work
- **공휴일** = public holiday

### Verbs
- **겹치다** = to overlap with, to coincide with
- **책임지다** = to be responsible for

### Adjectives
- **존경스럽다** = to be respectful

### Adverbs
- **때로** = sometimes

## Introduction

~(으)로서 is used to indicate that someone does something as a person who holds a specific status. The translation is usually "as."

## ~(으)로서: As a Person with a Specific Status

### Examples
- 엄마로서 해야 할 일이 너무 많아서 때로는 힘들다 = As a mom, there are many things to do, so it's difficult sometimes
- 저는 그 사건의 검사로서 재판을 전담하기로 했습니다 = I decided to take full charge of that court case as the prosecutor
- 슬기는 축구팀의 주장으로서 결승전에서 큰 활약을 했다 = Seulgi played a big active role in the playoffs as the captain
- 가장으로서 가족을 책임져야 하기 때문에 어깨가 무겁다 = There is a lot of burden on the breadwinner because he/she is responsible for the family
- 미술작품에 대한 전문가로서 이 작품이 진품임을 인증합니다 = As a specialist on art, I certify that this piece of work is authentic
- 이순신 장군은 한국에서 가장 유명한 장군으로서 모든 사람이 존경하는 인물이다 = Yi Sun Shin is the figure respected by all as the most famous general in Korea
- 대통령으로서 이번 명절이 주말과 겹치기 때문에 대체공휴일로 지정하기로 했습니다 = As the president, I will designate the following Monday as a make-up holiday

### With adjectives (is known as)
- 그 사람은 경쟁자로서 아주 존경스럽다 = That person is very respectful as a competitor
- 아인슈타인은 과학자로서 제일 똑똑하다 = Einstein is known as the smartest scientist

### With particles
- 엄마로서의 삶은 내가 생각한 것보다 훨씬 어려웠다 = Life as a mom was much more difficult than I thought
- 저의 새 언니가 아내로서는 좋지만 엄마로서는 별로예요 = My sister-in-law is good as a wife, but not as a mother
