## Introduction

~았/었어야 하다 indicates that one **should have** done something in the past but didn't. Adding ~았/었 before ~아/어야 creates this meaning.

## ~았/었어야 하다: One Should Have

- 몸살이 나기 전에 병원에 갔어야 해요 = You should have gone to the hospital before your body started aching
- 달빛이 예뻐서 사진을 찍었어야 해요 = The moon was so pretty, I should have taken a picture
- 수돗물 말고 애기한테 생수를 먹였어야 해요 = You should have fed the baby mineral water instead of tap water
- 집 계약을 사인을 했을 때 중개인과 상담했어야 해요 = When I signed the contract, I should have consulted with an intermediary
- 저녁을 먹으려면 면을 냉동실에서 미리 꺼내 해동했어야 해요 = I should have pulled the noodles out of the freezer in advance

### More natural with past tense: ~았/었어야 했어요
The same sentences above sound more natural when 하다 is also conjugated to past tense.

### ~았/었어야지 (colloquial, slightly sassy tone)
- 강아지가 음식을 핥기 전에 미리 치웠어야지 = You should have cleaned up the food in advance so the puppy wouldn't lick it
- 고혈압 환자라서 나트륨이 많이 들어간 음식을 줄였어야지 = Because you are a high blood pressure patient, you should have decreased foods with a lot of salt
