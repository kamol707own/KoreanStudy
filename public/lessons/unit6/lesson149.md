## Introduction

하필 is used in situations where there could have been many possible outcomes but the absolute worst one ended up happening. Best translation: "Of all..."

## 하필 – Of All Possible Situations

- 하필 택배가 지금 왔어? = Of all times for the package to arrive, it had to be right now?!
- 숙제를 처음으로 안 했는데 하필 오늘 시험을 봐야 돼 = Of all days to have a test, it's the day that I don't do the homework for the first time
- 오늘은 놀이공원에 가는 날인데 하필 감기에 걸린 것처럼 몸이 안 좋아 = Of all days for me to feel like I have a cold, it happens on the day we're going to the amusement park
- 하필 이번에 나침반을 안 챙겼네 = Of all times for me to forget my compass, it had to be this time!
- 하필 오늘 그 헌책방만 문을 닫았어 = Of all things to be closed, it had to be the used book store
- 집들이하는 날이 하필 엄마 생일과 겹쳐서 집들이를 취소했어 = Of all days, the housewarming party fell on the same day as mom's birthday
- 신선한 바람을 쐬러 시간을 내서 나왔는데 하필 비가 오기 시작했어 = I came outside for fresh air and, of all things, it started raining
- 하필 노래를 흥얼거리고 있는데 친구가 들어와서 얼굴이 화끈거렸어 = Of all things, I was humming when my friend came in and my face got all red
- 하필 밥을 다 차리고 먹기 전에 전화가 와서 밥이 다 식었어 = I got all the food ready and right before eating, of all things, the phone rang and the food got cold
- 새로 산 신발을 신고 나왔는데 하필 산길을 걸어서 신발이 더러워졌어 = I bought new shoes and, of all places, walked on a mountain trail so my shoes got dirty
