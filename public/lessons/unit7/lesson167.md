# Lesson 167: ~ㄹ/을까 말까 – Hesitating or Deciding

## Vocabulary

### Nouns

- **전설** = legend
- **금일** = today
- **식도** = esophagus (역류성 식도염 = acid reflux)
- **열매** = fruit (열매를 따다 = to pick fruit)
- **여든** = eighty
- **근거** = grounds, basis
- **출혈** = bleeding (내출혈 = internal bleeding)
- **내성적** = introverted
- **외향적** = extroverted
- **송별회** = farewell party

### Verbs

- **재생하다** = to relive, to revitalize
- **경매하다** = to auction
- **금주하다** = to give up drinking
- **송별하다** = to depart, to leave

### Adjectives

- **붉다** = to be red
- **색다르다** = to be different/unconventional
- **신비롭다** = to be marvelous/mysterious
- **상쾌하다** = to be refreshing

## A Review of 말다 and ~ㄹ/을까

- 말다 = to stop or avoid an action
- ~ㄹ/을까 = indicates thinking about doing an action

## ~ㄹ/을까 말까 – Hesitating or Deciding

This indicates one is hesitating or deciding whether to do an action. These sentences typically end with 고민하다.

- 건강을 위해 **금주할까 말까** 고민 중이에요 = I'm debating whether to quit drinking for my health or not
- 일을 **마무리할까 말까** 아직 고민 중이에요 = I'm still debating whether to finish the work or not
- 색다른 머리 스타일로 **바꿀까 말까** 고민 중이에요 = I'm debating whether to change my hairstyle to something different or not
- 이곳은 정말 신비로운데 사진을 **찍을까 말까** 고민 중이에요 = This place is really mysterious, so I'm debating whether to take a picture or not
- 별로 사이가 안 좋은 친구의 송별회에 **갈까 말까** 고민 중이에요 = I am debating whether I will go to the party of the friend that I'm not very close with
- 저는 원래 내성적인데 오늘은 외향적인 척을 **할까 말까** 고민 중이에요 = I'm usually introverted, but I'm debating whether to act extroverted today or not
- 이 옷이 너무 붉은데 **입을까 말까** 고민되네요 = This outfit is too red, so I'm debating whether to wear it or not
- 출혈이 심한데 병원에 **갈까 말까** 고민하고 있어요 = I'm bleeding a lot, but I'm debating whether to go to the hospital or not
- 그 용의자를 **심문할까 말까** 경찰이 고민하고 있어요 = The police are debating whether to interrogate the suspect or not
- 이 바람이 정말 상쾌한데 창문을 더 **열까 말까** 고민돼요 = The breeze is really refreshing, so I'm debating whether to open the window more or not
- 이 열매가 맛있어 보이긴 하는데 **먹을까 말까** 고민되네요 = This fruit looks delicious, but I'm debating whether to eat it or not
- 뜨거운 음식을 먹으면 식도가 아플 것 같은데 **먹을까 말까** 고민돼요 = My esophagus might hurt if I eat hot food, so I'm debating whether to eat it or not

### With other words indicating thinking/deciding

- 여든 살이 넘었지만 다시 한번 여행을 **떠날까 말까** 생각 중이에요 = I'm over eighty, but I'm thinking about whether to go on another trip or not
- 금일 경매에 **참여할까 말까** 아직 결정 못 했어요 = I haven't decided yet whether to participate in the auction today or not
- 이 물건을 **경매할까 말까** 아직 결정을 못 내렸어요 = I haven't decided yet whether to auction this item or not
