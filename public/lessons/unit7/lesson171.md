# Lesson 171: ~다 싶다: Feeling, Thought or Perception

## Vocabulary

### Nouns

- **간** = for a food to be salted appropriately (간이 맞다 = to be seasoned right)
- **국물** = soup broth
- **한입** = one bite, one mouthful
- **방문** = door to a room
- **경사** = slope (급경사 = steep slope)
- **황사** = yellow dust
- **지시** = order, directions
- **손실액** = amount of money lost
- **말소리** = voice
- **근무지** = workplace
- **꽃가루** = pollen
- **새끼손가락** = pinky finger

### Verbs

- **싱긋하다** = to grin
- **손실하다** = to lose
- **욱신거리다** = to ache, to throb

### Adjectives

- **한적하다** = to be secluded, to be quaint
- **매콤하다** = to be spicy
- **짭짤하다** = to be salty

### Adverbs

- **금세** = shortly, a short time later
- **다소** = somewhat

## ~다 싶다: Stating One's Feeling, Thought or Perception

By placing 싶다 after ~다 of a verb, adjective or 이다, the speaker indicates an idea they are not totally sure about, but something they think or perceive to be true.

- 새로운 근무지가 생각보다 **괜찮다 싶었어요** = I felt that my new workplace was better than expected
- 하늘을 보니 황사가 **심하다 싶었어요** = Looking at the sky, I felt that the yellow dust was severe
- 국물 맛을 보니 생각보다 **매콤하다 싶었어요** = After tasting the soup, I thought it was spicier than expected
- 본부에서 내린 지시가 다소 **엄격하다 싶었어요** = The order from headquarters seemed a bit strict
- 반찬을 한입 먹어 보니 간이 **짭짤하다 싶었어요** = After taking a bite of the side dish, I felt it was quite salty
- 방문 너머에서 들리는 말소리가 **불안하다 싶었어요** = The voice I heard beyond the door seemed uneasy
- 친구가 싱긋하는 것을 보니 좋은 소식이 **있다 싶었어요** = Seeing my friend grin, I thought there was good news
- 꽃가루가 날려서 오늘은 마스크를 **써야겠다 싶었어요** = Since pollen was floating around, I thought I should wear a mask today
- 예상보다 많은 돈을 손실해서 투자 판단이 **잘못되었다 싶었어요** = Since I lost more money than expected, I felt my investment decision was wrong
- 새끼손가락이 욱신거려서 **다쳤다 싶었어요** = My pinky finger was throbbing, so I must have injured it somehow
- 길이 꽤 가파른 경사라서 **조심해야겠다 싶었어요** = Since the road had a pretty steep slope, I thought I should be careful

### Describing upcoming nouns with 싶다

- 국물을 한입 마시니 조금 **짭짤하다 싶은 맛**이 났어요 = After taking a sip of the soup, it had a taste that seemed a bit salty
- 한적한 시골 마을을 보니 **여유롭다 싶은 분위기**가 느껴졌어요 = Looking at the quiet countryside village, I felt a relaxed atmosphere

### With other grammatical principles

- **~다 싶어서**: 길이 미끄럽다 싶어서 천천히 걸었어요 = Since I thought the road was slippery, I walked slowly
- **~다 싶으면**: 하늘을 보고 맑다 싶으면 금세 구름이 끼었어요 = Just when I thought the sky was clear, clouds quickly gathered
- **~다 싶을까**: 비가 그쳤다 싶을까 해서 우산을 놓고 나갔어요 = Thinking the rain had stopped, I went out without an umbrella
- **~다 싶은데**: 일이 거의 다 끝났다 싶은데 이제 집에 가는 게 어때요? = Since it feels like most of the work is done, how about going home now?
