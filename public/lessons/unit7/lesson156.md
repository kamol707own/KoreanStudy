# Lesson 156: ~(으)려는 참 and ~(으)려던 참 – One was just about to

## Vocabulary

### Nouns

- **돌** = rock
- **전세계** = whole world
- **쓰레기통** = garbage can
- **삼시** = three meals in a day (삼시 세끼 = three meals a day)
- **예고** = notice, warning
- **은퇴식** = retirement party
- **칼날** = blade of a knife
- **창공** = blue sky
- **여름날** = summer day
- **경로** = path for a person or object

### Verbs

- **은퇴하다** = to retire
- **소꿉장난하다** = to play house
- **떼다** = to remove something stuck
- **병들다** = to get sick, to catch a disease
- **씌우다** = to cover somebody else
- **쏟아지다** = to spill out

### Adverbs

- **참** = about to do
- **흠뻑** = wet

## ~(으)려는 참: To be about to do

참 is a pseudo-noun that can only be used when described by something. When described by ~(으)려는 or ~(으)려던, it indicates one was just about to do an action.

- 캠핑에서 먹을 삼시 세끼를 **준비하려는 참이에요** = I am about to prepare three meals a day for when we go camping
- **은퇴식이 시작되는 참이에요** = The retirement party is just about to start

### With ~에 (indicating another event happened as one was about to act)

- 창공을 **바라보려는 참에** 비행기가 지나갔어요 = I was about to look at the blue sky when a plane passed by
- **은퇴하려는 참에** 새로운 프로젝트 제안을 받았어요 = I was about to retire early when I received a new project proposal
- 스티커를 **떼려는 참에** 종이가 찢어졌어요 = I was about to remove the sticker when the paper tore
- 돌을 치우려는 참에 비가 오기 시작했어요 = I was about to remove the rock when it started to rain
- 쓰레기통을 **비우려던 참에** 전화가 왔어요 = I was about to empty the garbage can when the phone rang
- 칼날을 **갈려던 참에** 전기가 나갔어요 = I was about to sharpen the blade when the power went out
- 강아지가 병들려는 참에 병원에 데려갔어요 = The puppy was about to get sick when I took him to the hospital
- 비가 갑자기 쏟아져서 옷이 흠뻑 젖으려던 참에 친구가 우산을 씌워 줬어요 = I was about to get soaking wet when my friend covered me with an umbrella
- 아이들이 소꿉장난을 하려던 참에 엄마가 저녁을 먹으라고 불렀어요 = The children were about to play "house" when they were called for dinner

### With ~인데 (indicating information the listener will need)

- 전세계를 여행하려는 참인데, 여행 경로에 대한 정보가 더 필요해요 = I am about to travel the whole world, but I need more information about travel routes
- 여름날에 해변에 가려던 참인데 갑자기 비가 왔어요 = I was about to go to the beach on a summer day, but it suddenly started raining

### With ~니 (giving background information)

- 중요한 예고를 하려는 참이니 모두 집중해 주세요 = I am about to make an important announcement, so please pay attention

### With ~인 듯 (indicating it seems like an action will happen)

- 돌을 치우려는 참인 듯 힘을 주어 돌을 들어올렸어요 = He seemed to be about to remove the rock, and he lifted it with effort
- 중요한 예고를 하려던 참인 듯 그 남자는 잠시 망설였어요 = That man seemed to be about to make an important announcement, and he hesitated for a moment
