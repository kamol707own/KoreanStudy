# Lesson 168: ~거나 말거나: Indifference

## Vocabulary

### Nouns

- **내장** = intestines
- **흐름** = flow (현금의 흐름 = cash flow)
- **물개** = seal (animal)
- **교직** = teaching profession (교직원 = teacher)
- **배송비** = shipping fee
- **주사위** = dice
- **관심사** = concern
- **밤하늘** = night sky

### Verbs

- **쉬하다** = to pee (childish word)
- **심사하다** = to screen
- **격리하다** = to quarantine
- **개의하다** = to mind, to bother (개의치 않다 = to not mind)
- **상영하다** = to show a movie

### Adjectives

- **벌겋다** = for a face to be red/flushed
- **뿌옇다** = to be foggy/gray/murky/cloudy
- **어이없다** = to be absurd
- **부담스럽다** = to be burdensome

### Adverbs

- **별** = particular, real
- **반짝** = shiny
- **어떻든** = either way
- **더듬더듬** = stutter

## ~거나 말거나: Stating Indifference to a Situation

By placing ~거나 말거나 after an action, you can indicate indifference — it doesn't matter if that action occurs or not. Often ends with 상관없다, 개의치 않다, or other "I don't care" endings.

### Common endings: "it doesn't matter" / "I don't care"

- 날씨가 **뿌옇거나 말거나** 아무래도 **상관없어요** = Whether the weather is foggy or not, it really doesn't matter
- 주사위를 **던지거나 말거나** 아무래도 **상관없어요** = Whether I roll the dice or not, it really doesn't matter
- 백신을 **접종하거나 말거나** 어떻든 저는 **상관 안 해요** = Whether I get vaccinated or not, either way, I don't care
- 물개를 **보거나 말거나** 그건 **문제가 아니에요** = Whether I see a seal or not, that's not a problem
- 격리를 **하거나 말거나** 저한테는 **별 차이 없어요** = Whether I quarantine or not, it makes no real difference to me
- 내장을 **먹거나 말거나** 그것은 **중요하지 않아요** = Whether I eat intestines or not, that's not what's important
- 이 일이 **부담스럽거나 말거나** 그건 **문제가 안 돼요** = Whether this task is burdensome or not, that's not a problem
- 흐름을 **따르거나 말거나** 저는 별로 **개의치 않아요** = Whether I follow the flow or not, I don't really mind
- 별이 **반짝이거나 말거나** 저한테는 **중요하지 않아요** = Whether the stars are shining or not, it's not important to me
- 그가 **더듬더듬 말하거나 말거나** 그건 제 **관심사가 아니에요** = Whether he stutters or not, that's not my concern
- 얼굴이 **벌뻘거나 말거나** 저는 별로 **개의치 않아요** = Whether my face turns red or not, I don't really mind
- 영화를 **상영하거나 말거나** 그게 **중요한 게 아니에요** = Whether they show the movie or not, that's not what's important
- 제가 **교직에 있거나 말거나** 그건 제 **관심사가 아니에요** = Whether I'm in the teaching profession or not, that's not my concern

### Ending with a different action

- 흐름을 **따르거나 말거나** 결국엔 목표에 **도달할 거예요** = Whether I follow the flow or not, I will eventually reach my goal
- 별이 **반짝이거나 말거나** 밤하늘은 **아름다워요** = Whether the stars are shining or not, the night sky is beautiful
- 이유가 **어이없거나 말거나** 규칙은 **지켜야 해요** = Whether the reason is absurd or not, the rules must be followed
- 배송비를 **내거나 말거나** 저는 꼭 이 물건을 **살 거예요** = Whether I pay the shipping fee or not, I will definitely buy this item
- 지원자를 **심사하거나 말거나** 기준은 **똑같아야 해요** = Whether they screen the applicants or not, the standards should be the same
- 격리를 **하거나 말거나** 감기에 걸리지 않도록 **조심하세요** = Whether you quarantine or not, be careful not to catch a cold
