## Indicating an Emotion after hearing what somebody says: ~다니

**~다니** is a **compounded quoted grammatical principle**. The format is: the speaker repeats something that another person said (before ~다니 as the quoted part), and then indicates his/her emotion or feelings as a result of hearing that fact.

The ~다~ represents an abbreviation of ~ㄴ/는다고 하다 (quoting).

### Examples:

- 네가 **떠나다니** 너무 슬퍼 = I'm sad that you are leaving
- 너의 아버지도 **오시다니** 기뻐 = I'm glad that your dad is coming as well
- 너의 부모님이 **이혼하시다니** 안됐다 = It's too bad that your parents are getting a divorce
- 네가 나에게 **고맙다니** 내가 더 고맙다 = After hearing you say 'thank you' to me, I feel more thankful
- 슬기가 대학원에 **가다니** 믿을 수 없어 = I can't believe Seulgi is going to graduate school
- 북극에 있는 빙하가 다 **녹다니** 큰 일이다 = It is a big problem that all the glaciers in the north pole are melting
- 우리 집 앞에 경찰서가 **생기다니** 다행이다 = Thankfully a police station is being built in front of our house
- 해양 생물이 점점 **사라지고 있다니** 믿을 수가 없다 = I can't believe that the organisms in the ocean are gradually disappearing
- 부모님의 허락도 없이 쌍꺼풀 수술을 **하다니** 정말 실망이다 = I'm disappointed that you would get a double eyelid surgery without the permission of your parents
- 아이들은 추워도 아이스크림을 많이 **먹는 경향이 있다니** 신기하다 = It's crazy that kids have the tendency to eat ice cream even when they are cold

### With past tense:

- 네가 **떠났다니** 너무 슬퍼 = I'm sad that you left
- 슬기가 대학원에 **갔다니** 믿을 수 없다 = I can't believe Seulgi went to graduate school
- 너의 부모님이 **이혼하셨다니** 안됐다 = It's too bad that your parents got a divorce
- 너의 강아지가 **죽었다니** 너무 안됐다 = It is too bad that your dog died
- 놀이터에 있는 그네가 **망가졌다니** 믿을 수 없다 = I can't believe that the swing in the playground broke

## ~ㄴ/는다니 (future tense)

**~ㄴ/는다니** has the same meaning as ~다니, except the quoted part is in the **future tense**:

- 슬기가 대학원에 **간다니** 믿을 수 없어요 = I can't believe Seulgi will go to graduate school
- 너의 부모님이 **이혼하신다니** 안됐다 = It's too bad that your parents will get a divorce
- 우리 집 앞에 경찰서가 **생긴다니** 다행이다 = Thankfully a police station will be built in front of our house
- 북극에 있는 빙하가 다 **녹는다니** 큰 일이다 = It is a big problem that all the glaciers in the north pole will melt
- 학교 후문을 내일부터 이용 못 **한다니** 짜증난다 = It's annoying that we won't be able to use the back door starting from tomorrow
- 집 앞에 있는 오래된 단풍나무를 잘라야 **한다니** 너무 안타까워요 = It is too bad that we will have to cut the old maple tree in front of our house
