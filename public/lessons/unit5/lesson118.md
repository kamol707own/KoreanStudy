## Stating a Fact from Experience: ~더라

**~더라** is formed from **~더~** + **~라**. When used, the speaker is expressing some fact that he/she **witnessed and knows from first-hand experience**.

A good translation: **"from my own experience, I realized/saw/noticed that…"**

### Comparison:

- 가방이 무거워 = The bag is heavy (simply stating a fact)
- 가방이 **무겁더라** = From my own experience, I realized that the bag is heavy

### Examples with adjectives:

- 이민호는 정말 **멋지더라** = Minho Lee is very stylish (from what I experienced)
- 캐나다 날씨가 너무 **춥더라** = I realized that Canadian weather is very cold
- 한국말을 배우는 게 **힘들더라** = I realized that learning Korean is hard
- 그 청소년은 여드름이 **많더라** = I saw/noticed that that kid has a lot of acne
- 그 선생님이 수업을 할 때 열정이 **가득하더라** = I saw/noticed that teacher is full of passion
- 그 차고에 여러 가지 물건이 가득 쌓여 **있더라** = I saw/noticed that there are a lot of things piled up in the garage
- 스마트 TV만 있으면 핸드폰으로 다 **조정할 수 있더라** = I noticed that if you have smart TV, you can control everything with your phone

### Examples with verbs and 이다:

- 문제는 컴퓨터**더라** = From my experience, I realized the problem is the computer
- 이 고기는 국내산**이더라** = From my experience, I realized this meat is from Korea
- 그 학생이 뻥을 많이 **치더라** = From my experience, that student jokes a lot
- 콘센트가 침대와 책상 가운데에 **있더라** = From my experience, the outlet is between the bed and the desk
- 부장님이 아파트에서 안 살고 한옥에서 **살더라** = From my experience, the boss lives in a Korean traditional house

### Past tense before ~더라:

By saying 어제 비가 **오더라** you are saying that you personally saw it raining. By saying 어제 비가 **왔더라** you are saying that you saw that it had rained (but didn't see it actually rain).

- 어제 비가 **오더라** = I saw it rain yesterday
- 어제 비가 **왔더라** = I saw that it had rained yesterday
- 여자친구가 식당에서 밥을 **먹더라** = I saw my girlfriend eating at the restaurant
- 여자친구가 식당에서 밥을 다 **먹었더라** = I saw that my girlfriend had eaten all of her food

### Asking questions with ~더라:

Used when the speaker knew something but can't remember:

- 빵이 얼마였**더라**? = How much did the bread cost, again? (I can't remember)
- 슬기가 어디 **갔더라**? = Ah, where did Seulgi go, again?
- 내가 언제 **졸업했더라**? = Ah, when did I graduate again?
- 종이를 거실 어디다가 **두었더라**? = Where did I put the paper in the living room again?
