# Lesson 13: Korean Particles: and, with, to, from, for, about

Source: [How to Study Korean - Unit 1, Lesson 13](https://www.howtostudykorean.com/unit1/unit-1-lessons-9-16/lesson-13/)

## Particles for "And" and "With": ~과/와, ~랑/이랑, ~하고

All three pairs can be used interchangeably:

| Form | After vowel | After consonant |
|------|------------|----------------|
| 과/와 | 와 | 과 |
| 랑/이랑 | 랑 | 이랑 |
| 하고 | 하고 | 하고 |

**As "and" (between two nouns):**
- 사과와 바나나 = apples and bananas
- 밥과 빵 = rice and bread

**As "with":**
- 친구와 갔어요 = I went with my friend
- 아버지랑 공원에 갈 거야 = I will go to the park with my dad
- 학생들과 박물관에 갔다 = The teacher went to the museum with the students

> ~과/와 is more formal/written; ~랑/이랑 is more spoken

**With 같이/함께 (together):**
- 빵을 같이 먹었어요 = We ate bread together
- 음악을 함께 들었어요 = We listened to music together

## Particles for "To" (giving): 에게/한테/께

| Particle | Usage |
|----------|-------|
| 한테 | Spoken/informal |
| 에게 | Written (also spoken) |
| 께 | Honorific (to someone deserving respect) |

- 아버지는 아들에게 돈을 준다 = The father gives money to his son
- 학생들한테 한국어를 가르쳤어 = I taught Korean to the students
- 부장님께 말할 거예요 = I will tell the boss

## Particles for "From": 에게서/한테서/(으)로부터

- 여자친구에게서 편지를 받았어요 = I received a letter from my girlfriend
- 친구한테서 들었어요 = I heard that from my friend
- 정부로부터 받았어요 = I received from the government

> ~(으)로부터 can be used with non-persons; 에게서/한테서 only with persons

## For Somebody: ~을/를 위해(서)

- 여자 친구를 위해 꽃을 샀어 = I bought flowers for my girlfriend
- 부장님을 위해 이것을 썼어 = I wrote this for my boss
- 회사를 위해 열심히 일할 거예요 = I will work hard for the company

## About Something: ~에 대해

- 너에 대해 생각했어 = I thought about you
- 아버지에 대해 말했어 = I spoke about my father
- 그것에 대해 책을 쓸 거야 = I will write a book about it

> ~에 대해 and ~에 대해서 are interchangeable
