# Lesson 20: Korean Tenses and Formality Overview

Source: [How to Study Korean - Unit 1, Lesson 20](https://www.howtostudykorean.com/unit1/unit-1-lessons-17-25-2/lesson-20/)

## Review of All Tenses

### Verbs

| Tense | Plain form | Informal low | Informal high | Formal high |
|-------|-----------|-------------|---------------|-------------|
| Present | 먹는다 | 먹어 | 먹어요 | 먹습니다 |
| Past | 먹었다 | 먹었어 | 먹었어요 | 먹었습니다 |
| Future | 먹겠다 | 먹겠어 | 먹겠어요 | 먹겠습니다 |
| Future (will) | 먹을 것이다 | 먹을 거야 | 먹을 거예요 | 먹을 것입니다 |

### Adjectives

| Tense | Plain form | Informal low | Informal high | Formal high |
|-------|-----------|-------------|---------------|-------------|
| Present | 크다 | 커 | 커요 | 큽니다 |
| Past | 컸다 | 컸어 | 컸어요 | 컸습니다 |
| Future | 크겠다 | 크겠어 | 크겠어요 | 크겠습니다 |

## Irregular Review

| Irregular | Rule | Example |
|-----------|------|---------|
| ㅅ | ㅅ removed before vowel | 짓다 → 지어 |
| ㄷ | ㄷ→ㄹ before vowel | 걷다 → 걸어 |
| ㅂ | ㅂ→우/오 before vowel | 춥다 → 추워 |
| ㅡ | ㅡ removed, look at previous vowel | 크다 → 커 |
| 르 | ㄹ→ㄹㄹ | 빠르다 → 빨라요 |
| ㄹ | ㄹ removed before ㄴ/ㅂ/ㅅ | 알다 → 압니다 |

## Formality Levels Summary

| Level | Ending | Usage |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Plain/diary | ~다 | Books, tests, articles |
| Informal low | ~어/아 | Friends, family |
| Informal high | ~어요/아요 | Most situations (default polite) |
| Formal high | ~습니다/ㅂ니다 | Very formal situations |

> **Default choice:** When in doubt, use informal high (~어요/아요). It's polite enough for almost any situation.
