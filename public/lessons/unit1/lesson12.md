# Lesson 12: Korean Particles 들, 만, 에서, 부터, 까지, (으)로

Source: [How to Study Korean - Unit 1, Lesson 12](https://www.howtostudykorean.com/unit1/unit-1-lessons-9-16/lesson-12/)

## Particle ~들 (Plural)

~들 makes a noun plural, but is usually only used with people:

- 의사들 = doctors
- 학생들 = students
- 배우들 = actors

> Korean rarely distinguishes singular/plural. 나는 사과를 샀어 = I bought an apple(s)

## Using 몇 (Some/How many)

몇 replaces a number with counters to mean "some":

- 사과 몇 개 = some apples
- 친구 몇 명 = some friends
- 몇 번 = a few times

## Particle ~만 (Only)

~만 means "only" and replaces subject/object particles:

| Korean | English |
|--------|---------|
| 물만 마셔 | I only drink water |
| 나는 그 여자만 좋아해 | I only like that girl |
| 사과 한 개만 샀어 | I only bought one apple |
| 공부만 했어요 | I only studied |

**With location particles:**
- 학교에만 갔어요 = We only went to school
- 밤에만 운동해요 = I exercise only at night

## Particle ~에서 (From/At)

~에서 indicates the location where an action occurs:

| Example | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| 학교에서 공부할 거예요 | I will study at school |
| 병원에서 봤어요 | I saw at the hospital |
| 한국에서 배웠어요 | I learned in Korea |

**Also means "from":**
- 캐나다에서 왔어요 = I come from Canada
- 서울역에서 내릴 거예요 = I will get off at Seoul station

> Note: ~에서 is NOT used with 있다. Use ~에 instead.

## Particles ~부터 and ~까지 (From...to/Until)

~부터 = from (starting point)
~까지 = to/until (end point)

| Example | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| 3시까지 기다릴 거예요 | I will wait until 3:00 |
| 어제부터 아팠어요 | I have been sick since yesterday |
| 아침부터 밤까지 공부만 했어요 | From morning to night I only studied |
| 처음부터 끝까지 | From start to finish |

## Particle ~(으)로 (With/By/In)

~(으)로 has multiple meanings:

**1. Tool/method/material:**
- 펜으로 썼어요 = I wrote with a pen
- 차로 갔어요 = I went by car
- 나무로 지었어요 = I built out of wood

**2. Language:**
- 한국어로 말했어요 = I spoke in Korean
- 영어로 할 거예요 = I will do it in English

**3. Direction:**
- 집으로 갈 거예요 = I will go home
- 오른쪽으로 가세요 = Go right

> ~로 after vowels, ~으로 after consonants (except ㄹ)
